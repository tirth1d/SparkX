Who we are?

Team SparkX is a group of graduate and undergraduate students from Silver Oak College Of Technology, Ahmedabad, Gujarat. We design and manufacture an All-Terrain Vehicle which participates in Baja SAE India, Enduro Student India and Baja SAE International. We design, manufacture, test and race our ATV with only one aim in mind: Victory. We believe, ”We never lose. We either Win or Learn.”

What we've done?

We started making ATVs way back in 2013 and till now we’ve made total of 6 different versions of ATV’s. Every version has new features in it and comes with some major OR minor upgrades as compare to its previous versions like less weight, high acceleration, high speed etc. We still have been working on this features to improve them as much as possible and to win the first prize in every competition we participate. We always believe, “Improvement is better than delayed Perfection.”

Our Journey

The journey of Team SparkX began way back in 2013 when a group of 10 undergraduates dared to do something different. They came up with an idea of forming a team which will build an All-Terrain Vehicle (ATV) to compete in the prestigious competition of BAJA SAE India. A team was formed and the name was inspired by SpaceX and its CEO ‘Elon Musk’-Anything would be less to describe him.
